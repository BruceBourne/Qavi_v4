# ◈ Qavi
## *Every rupee. One place. Complete clarity.*

Qavi is a private wealth management platform built for serious investors and the advisors who serve them. Stop switching between apps, spreadsheets, and broker statements. Everything you own — from Nifty 50 stocks and mutual funds to government bonds, fixed deposits, and gold — tracked, analysed, and presented in one beautifully simple interface.

---

## What You Get

**Your entire portfolio. Instantly clear.**

The moment you log in, you see the full picture. Invested capital, current value, total P&L, and allocation across every asset class. Not yesterday's numbers — today's closing prices, pulled automatically.

**Every asset class India offers.**

Equities · Mutual Funds · ETFs · Government Bonds · PSU Bonds · NCDs · Sovereign Gold Bonds · Bank FDs · Corporate FDs · Gold · Silver. All in one portfolio, analysed together.

**Holdings that actually make sense.**

Every holding shows what you paid, what it's worth today, and how much you've made or lost — in rupees and percentage. Sort, filter, drill down. One click to see a full 5-year return history, Sharpe ratio, and max drawdown on any individual asset.

**Work with a financial advisor who gets it.**

Qavi is built for the advisor-client relationship. Your advisor manages your portfolios, shares performance reports, and schedules consultations — all through the platform. You see exactly what your advisor sees. Nothing hidden, nothing confusing.

**Schedule consultations in seconds.**

Request a meeting with your advisor directly from the platform. Choose your preferred time, and your advisor confirms. Meeting link, calendar invite, and notes — all in one place. Whether you need a quick portfolio review or a deep dive into your financial goals, it starts right here.

**Invoices that are actually professional.**

Advisors generate clean, landscape A4 invoices with full portfolio breakdowns. One-time fees, consultation billing, AUM-based management fees — all calculated correctly and sent as downloadable documents. Clients never have to chase for a statement.

- See your complete financial picture across every broker, every asset class

- Create shared portfolios your clients can view, and private portfolios only you see

- Schedule and track all client meetings in one place
- Analyse fee strategy across your entire book with the Fee Analyser
- See which clients are growing fastest and where the opportunity lies
