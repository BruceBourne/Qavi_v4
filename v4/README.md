# ◈ Qavi

### *Wealth. Clarity. Control.*

A private, invite-only wealth management platform for investors — tracking every investment across equities, mutual funds, ETFs, bonds, fixed deposits, gold, and silver in one place.

---

## What Qavi Does

**For Investors**

See your complete financial picture in one dashboard. Every rupee you've invested — where it is, what it's worth today, and how it's performing. Allocation breakdowns, P&L per holding, benchmark comparison, and goal tracking, all without switching between apps or spreadsheets.

Drill into any individual asset for its full history — 1-day to 5-year returns, volatility, and key ratios. Create private portfolios visible only to you. Request meetings with your advisor directly through the platform.

**Markets**

Live prices across Indian markets — equities, mutual funds, ETFs, bonds, government schemes, bank and corporate fixed deposits, gold, and silver. Updated automatically during market hours.
